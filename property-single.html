Modern Condo in Doral

Welcome to 204 Mount Olive Road, a stylish 2-bedroom, 1-bathroom condo available for rent at $1,200 per month. Located in the vibrant community of Doral, Florida, this 850 sq ft unit offers a perfect blend of comfort and convenience.

The condo features an open-plan living area with large windows, allowing natural light to flood the space.
